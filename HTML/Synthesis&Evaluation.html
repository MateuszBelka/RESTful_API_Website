Introduction

In the "Synthesis & evaluation" assignment we showed strong sides of our website, revealed problems that we encountered, analyzed comments from our TA and defined how we can improve our website to follow main guidelines. Our evaluation consists of 3 main parts: Introduction, Website's accessibility and mistakes and Appendices A(Guidelines' evaluation), B(Evaluation based on TA's comments).

Accessibility highlights

We have done our best to make our website convenient for using it for both workers and users. Our website provides a navigation bar on the top of the webpage including "Products", "Location", "Service", "For business" and information "About" our company. Our "Bestsellers" table provides forms such that it will be convenient for web-developers and workers to update products. At the end of our page, there is a link that jumps to the beginning of our page.

Improvement plan

Among all the improvements we have done, such as additional CSS tags that we missed in the first assignment and comments, describing the intention of each part of the code, we did not implement additional changes in our print version of the code. We believe that our print version alters the design of the page such that tables are located one below the other. Additionally, the general design of the website has been checked on print option in the browser to make sure that all elements of the website are properly displayed on the printed version. Therefore, we believe that our Print stylesheet has been properly adjusted for printing our webpage.
